W E L C O M E

GS Inn is a budget hotel located in Floridablanca, Pampanga, Philippines. It offers clean and comfortable accommodations for travelers on a budget. The hotel features a standard rooms, which are air-conditioned and equipped with cable TV, a private bathroom with shower, and free Wi-Fi access.


About

GS Inn is a great option for budget-conscious travelers seeking comfortable accommodations in Floridablanca, Pampanga.


Geraldine Sagad, a member of the Sagad family, founded the GS Inn in 2014, which is a family-run enterprise with offices in Valdez, Floridablanca Pampanga. Because there aren't many hotels in the area, the owner of this company decided to build a facility just for families and couples looking for a quiet place to unwind and stay. GS Inn is the most affordable priced private hotel in town with clean and top-notch services. Options include short-term stays and overnight stays depending on the needs of the client.
